About GoDrive Nashik

GoDrive Nashik is a local car rental service based in Nashik, Maharashtra. Our goal is to provide clean, reliable, and affordable self-drive cars for daily use, road trips, or local travel.

Whether you're a tourist exploring the vineyards and temples of Nashik, or a local looking for flexible travel, our fleet includes top picks like the Swift, Swift Dzire, and Ertiga.

We are committed to quality service, timely support, and a smooth booking experience — all without the need for long paperwork or complicated processes. Just call, book, and drive!
